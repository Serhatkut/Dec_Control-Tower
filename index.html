🚨 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

  • OTD (On Time Delivery): Each parcel receives an internal Expected Delivery Date (EDD) constraint derived directly from target SLAs. A parcel rigidly fails OTD if its actual final-scan Delivered Timestamp misses that EDD deadline.
  • OTP (On Time Pick Up): Manifested parcels must be successfully scanned physically loaded within a rigid 24 Hour window.
  • NOP (Network On Time): Flagged negatively if a shipment absorbs a "Customs Hold" or "Network Transit Exception".

🔴 Anomaly Detection Algorithm (The Reds)

The internal anomaly engine deeply indexes all Active Pipeline Parcels continuously and scores volumetric failure ratios. It detects clusters of extreme stagnation and visually pulls the absolute worst-performing Customer Account, Origin Depot bottleneck, Mid-Mile linehaul Transit Route, or End-Hub Exception, guaranteeing Operations Managers instantly notice massive critical blackouts.
